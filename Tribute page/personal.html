Virat Kohli is married to Anushka Sharma, a prominent Bollywood actress. The couple got married in a private ceremony in Italy in December 2017. In January 2021, they welcomed their daughter, Vamika. Kohli has been private about his personal life, and the couple has often shared glimpses into their family life through social media.

Kohli has his own clothing line called Wrogn, which features a range of casual wear. He is also associated with various other brands and has been recognized as one of the most marketable athletes globally. In addition to his personal business ventures, Kohli has actively engaged in philanthropy. He, along with Anushka Sharma, has been involved in various charitable activities.
